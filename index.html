About

Audio Professional
Based in LA

Matt Dyson is a Los Angeles-based audio professional whose work spans multi-platinum albums, major-label releases, film, television, and podcast production. Currently serving as Head of Audio at Treefort Media, he brings the same level of craft and precision to every format he works in.

With credits ranging from Adele and Harry Styles to Killer Psyche and The Hive, Matt's background covers music recording and mixing, audio post production, sound design, and editorial — making him equally at home in a recording studio or a post suite.
